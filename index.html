Tilburg-CoDeRs

We are a RoboCup SPL team at the Department of Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence at Tilburg University. We are part of the Cognitive Developmental Robotics (CoDeRs) group at AIR-Lab. Our team name is a thoughtful nod to our origins within the CoDeRs-group, reflecting our deep connection to cognition and robotics.
